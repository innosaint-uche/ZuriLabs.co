Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of every member, partner, and visitor in the Zuri Labs ecosystem.

1. Information we collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, organisation, and interests when you join the community, register for events, or submit partnership enquiries.

We may also collect usage data such as device type, browser version, and pages visited to help us improve the experience.

2. How we use your information

Your information helps us deliver tailored programming, communicate updates, and coordinate partnerships. We may use anonymised data to analyse engagement trends and measure program impact.

We do not sell personal data. Where we share data with service providers (e.g. email distribution, analytics), we require appropriate privacy safeguards.

3. Cookies and tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain site functionality and may use analytics cookies to understand how our platforms are used. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

4. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to deliver services, meet legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You may request deletion or updates to your data at any time.

5. Your rights

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask for corrections, updates, or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact privacy@zuri.africa.

This policy will be updated as our programs evolve. The latest revision date is October 2024.
